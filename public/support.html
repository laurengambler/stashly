Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the Stashly app and tap "Sign Up." Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, email us at stashlyadmin@gmail.com from the email associated with your account. We'll process your request within 7 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

I found a bug or have a feature request

We'd love to hear from you! Send your feedback to stashlyadmin@gmail.com with as much detail as possible (device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the issue).
